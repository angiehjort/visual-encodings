We treat customer data with respect and care. We sign NDA if needed.

Xlsx, csv, tsv. As well as google or microsoft online spreadsheet

History

We grew our expertise about charts and data stories at Gapminder, a non-profit independent foundation on a mission of fighting ignorance with a fact-based worldview. To keep the foundation focused on the mission we had to turn down most of the commercial requests from companies and organizations who would like to use beautiful Gapminder animated graphs for their own purposes.

Visual Encodings AB is our separate for-profit company that helps you use these graphs with your own data and add customisations for your own stories. Or we can make new graphs for you.
