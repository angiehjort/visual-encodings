Questions and answers

How can i be sure my data is safe with you?
We treat customer data with respect and care
What does it cost?
Depends a lot on how complex is the data. The maintenance of the basic demo for 1 year costs 200 USD, beyond that there are multiple options with how the charts can be customised or developed for your particular case. That's why we need to see the data first to give you an offer with different price options.
What file formats do you support?
xlsx, csv, tsv, google or microsoft online spreadsheet
Will the demo keep working?
Yes for 3 weeks. This may be all you need, otherwise we offer a 1 year maintenance of the demo for a small fee

History

We grew our expertise about charts and data stories at Gapminder, a non-profit independent foundation on a mission of fighting ignorance with a fact-based worldview. To keep the foundation focused on the mission we had to turn down most of the commercial requests from companies and organizations who would like to use beautiful Gapminder animated graphs for their own purposes.

Visual Encodings AB is our separate for-profit company that helps you use these graphs with your own data and add customisations for your own stories. Or we can make new graphs for you.
